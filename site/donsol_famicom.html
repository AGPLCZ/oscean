    Donsol Famicom is a port of the dungeon-crawler card game for the classic Nintendo.

    Donsol is a dungeon crawler card game played with a standard deck of 54 playing cards. The game was programmed entirely in 6502 assembly, the art assets were created by rekka, the game was designed by John Eternal and the ROM will released by hundred rabbits.

    The game should be released in the spring of 2020. A cartridge release is being considered, stay tuned for more updates.
