Pino's new main sail - 18J05

pino

Pino is a Yamaha 33 sailboat built in 1982.

Purchased in 2016, on Vancouver Island, Pino has since sailed across the Pacific Ocean. We maintain a list of technical details, and our current position can be accessed through our tracker.

If you are interested in learning about the digital-nomad lifestyle, have a look at the Hundred Rabbits Website. We have a lot of raspberry-powered devices onboard, learn more about our media_station and radio_station.
