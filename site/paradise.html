ParadiseOS - 18S14

In Paradise, you are but a force acting upon places, objects, words — vessels.

Paradise is currently being expanded into an experimental shell, and file-system, for an upcoming light Linux distribution. The project also features an inline scripting language inspired from LISP, called lain.

Until then, in this anonymous world you can create anything, traverse vastly different universes, and share your world with others.
