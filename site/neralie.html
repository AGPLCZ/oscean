    Neralie is a decimal time format.

    The Neralie clock has two groups of 3 digits, called the beat & the pulse. A beat contains 1000 pulses, and equivalent to 86.4 seconds.

    • 6:00 250:000
    • 12:00 500:000
    • 18:00 750:000
