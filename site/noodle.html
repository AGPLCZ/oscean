    Noodle is a sketching tool.

    Noodle is a minimal 1bit drawing tool designed to be used alongside its companion tool Moogle. It offers a handful of line and fill styles, it was written in Plan9 C.

    Noodle was the first in a series of image creation tools, exploring the concept of Small Sharp Tools for graphical tasks, as an alternative take to the general purpose software Ronin.

    You can find a collection of drawings created with it here, you can also use it directly in your browser here.
