    Nasu is a spritesheet and nametable editor.

    Nasu is a spritesheet and nametable editor created to help us design and assemble the assets of our famicom games. It can import and export both .chr spritesheets and .asm nametables.

    The application was initially created to help us with the porting of donsol to the Famicom, and was later made available as a free and open source software.

    Learn more by reading the manual. If you need help, visit the Community. A version of Nasu is currently under development for Plan9, as chr9.
