    psychology

    A cheatsheet on Psychology.

    This is an edited version of PTFQ's Three Tiers of Mind.

    Intention
    ●●● Clings to the future Respects you Defined by intentions
    ●●○ Clings to the present Respects your status Defined by association
    ●○○ Clings to the past Respects your earnings Defined by the past
    Actions
    ●●● Talks of ideas Thinks in pictures Praises what you did
    ●●○ Talks of people Thinks in words Praises who you are
    ●○○ Talks of things Thinks in feelings Praises what you have
    Aspirations
    ●●● Wants to live forever Looks for equals Fears boredom
    ●●○ Wants to be remembered Looks for leaders Fears uncertainty
    ●○○ Wants to die happy Looks for gods Fears pain
    The world is a comedy to those that think, a tragedy to those that feel.
